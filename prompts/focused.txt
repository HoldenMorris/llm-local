Classify this email as SPAM or HAM.

SPAM characteristics:
- Contains "URGENT", "act now", "limited time", "expire"
- Claims you won something or have a prize
- Asks for passwords, credit cards, or personal info
- Has suspicious links or misspellings (amaz0n, g00gle)
- Pressure tactics or threats
- Too-good-to-be-true offers (70% off MacBooks)

HAM (legitimate) characteristics:
- Normal business communication
- Calendar invites, meeting notifications
- Shipping confirmations, security codes
- Known company notifications (GitHub, Slack, AWS, Zoom)

Answer with only: SPAM or HAM
